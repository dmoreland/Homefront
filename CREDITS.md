# Third-party assets

## Icons
Building, equipment, resource and force icons are from **game-icons.net**,
used under the **Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported (CC BY 3.0)** licence.
Icons by **Lorc**, **Delapouite**, and **sbed** — https://game-icons.net/
Recoloured (background removed, `currentColor` fill) for this project.

## Flags & insignia
National flag SVGs are public domain:
- UK, USA, France, Italy, Japan and the modern German tricolour from the
  **hampusborgos/country-flags** collection
  (https://github.com/hampusborgos/country-flags).
- The Soviet flag is the public-domain *Flag of the Soviet Union* from Wikimedia Commons.

The **modern** German flag is used deliberately: the game does **not** depict the
1933–1945 Nazi swastika flag or any extremist symbolism (per the project's design
guidelines). Germany's in-game insignia is the **Balkenkreuz** — the Wehrmacht/
Luftwaffe cross — period-accurate military marking without the party symbol.

Nation roundels (RAF/US/French/Italian roundels, Hinomaru, Balkenkreuz, Soviet red
star) are original inline SVG drawn for this project.
